BEST BEACHES IN VENEZUELA
LOS ROQUES

Los Roques is a natural paradise and one of the best-kept secrets on the Venezuelan coast. It is famous for its paradisiacal beaches and for having some of the most impressive underwater beds in the Caribbean. A visit to Los Roques offers the possibility of choosing between multiple cays and islets.

La Tortuga Island

La Tortuga Island is one of the most luxurious tourist destinations in Venezuela. This "paradise," as many call it, has no permanent inhabitants, which is why it is still considered a 'semi-virgin' island. It is truly splendid, although it is not that easy to get to.
